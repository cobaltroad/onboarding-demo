The solution: Customer Admin Portal

A self-service interface that gives customer admins direct control over:

  • Roster management – Create teams, invite users, assign roles, deactivate accounts
  • Usage monitoring – See seat consumption, active users, and team-level activity
  • Onboarding checklist – Guided steps from first login to full adoption

✅ Integrates with your existing OAuth—no new auth flows to build.
